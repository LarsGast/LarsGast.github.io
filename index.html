Home

Hello, and welcome to my website!

My name is Lars and in this site, I keep different bits of information regarding my journey in development. This includes projects, learning opportunities, and maybe even some open-ended writing. It is meant to serve as a place to store my thought process and to provide easy navigation to the different projects that I've worked on to highlight my skills in software development.

Use the navigation located in the header of each page to navigate throughout the website. Below is a short description of each main page and what they contain.

  • Home

    That's where we are right now! It's the landing page of this website and can be used as a hub to move to different parts of the site.

  • Articles

    A place where I write about whatever it is that's on my mind. This can be projects, learning opportunities, or something else entirely.

  • Projects

    Here, I document what projects I have worked on or am currently working on in a cv sort of style. Some projects might also have an article that explains my thought process and certain decisions that I have made.

  • About me

    If you want to know more about me, this is the place to be. On this page, I'll tell you who I am and how I got to where I am right now.

The site is very empty at this point in time. It also lacks styling and maybe some proper HTML semantics. The site is very young and my knowledge of HTML and CSS is still limited. Good thing the first article on this site will be about me learning just that.
